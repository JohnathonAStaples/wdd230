Target Audience

For the locals that so they know whats going on from a day to day basis. Also for those looking to move here to see what there is to offer or just for visitors so they can plan a road trip and a place to stop while passing thru.

Persona 1 As a farmer.

While as a farmer there are things that we need to know like when is the best time of the year to start our crops we will let you know here at the site. We will have links to our suppliers in town so it will be easier for you to find a local spot to shop at for seeds fertalizers and farm equpiment.It will also have a place for you to post on the site to do a farmers market so you can sell the fresh produce at a price that you can make money at verses the lower prices you get from a store.
